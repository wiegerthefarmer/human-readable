# Comic Prompt Template

Create a Human-Readable comic.

Title:
> [COMIC TITLE]

Seed sentence:
> [REAL OR OVERHEARD SENTENCE]

Premise:
> [SHORT PREMISE: why would someone say this?]

Format:
> [3-panel strip / 9-panel page / other]

Tone:
- dry
- observational
- technically literate
- lightly whimsical
- understated
- affectionate toward technical culture

Visual style:
- minimalist comic linework
- clear readable panels
- expressive but simple characters
- avoid clutter unless clutter is part of the joke
- preserve legible technical labels where important
- prefer one strong visual metaphor over several small gags

Story rule:
The seed sentence is source material, not necessarily the opening line. It should appear somewhere if it fits naturally.

The comic should answer:
> Why would someone say that?

Dialogue/script:
```text
[PASTE SCRIPT HERE]
```

Important constraints:
- Do not over-explain the joke.
- Keep the final beat understated.
- Prefer human/system mismatch over generic tech humor.
- Make invisible assumptions or technical culture visible.
- Whiteboards, terminals, diagrams, and labels may be used when helpful.
- AI characters should appear only if AI is part of the seed sentence, prompt, or scene.
