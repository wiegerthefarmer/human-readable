Create a Human-Readable comic.

Format:

Tone:
- dry
- observational
- technically literate
- lightly whimsical
- understated

Visual style:
- minimalist comic linework
- clear readable panels
- expressive but simple characters
- avoid clutter unless clutter is part of the joke
- preserve legible technical labels where important

Dialogue/script:
```text
[PASTE SCRIPT HERE]
```

Important constraints:

- Do not over-explain the joke.
- Keep the final beat understated.
- Prefer human/system mismatch over generic tech humor.
- Whiteboards, terminals, diagrams, and labels may be used when helpful.
