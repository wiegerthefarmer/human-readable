# Recurring Gags

## Cool Sysadmin

A calm, sunglasses-wearing sysadmin who appears completely unbothered by infrastructure disasters.

Core emotional state:

> He knows exactly how bad this is. He has simply moved beyond fear.

## Whiteboard Explanations

Characters frequently attempt to solve human problems by drawing technical diagrams.

The diagrams often make the problem clearer, but not necessarily better.

## Over-Specific Labels

Tiny technical labels can appear in diagrams, mugs, servers, or dashboards.

Examples:

- `probably-prod`
- `temporary-final-v3`
- `works-on-my-machine`
- `root cause pending`
- `do-not-reboot`
- `deprecated but load-bearing`

## The Mug

A recurring coffee mug may contain rotating slogans:

- Root Cause Coffee
- Caffeine as a Service
- Hash Browns
- Works on My Machine
- Strongly Eventually Consistent
