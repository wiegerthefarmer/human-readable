# Panel Layouts

## Three-Panel Strip

Default format for short observations.

Structure:

1. Setup
2. Escalation or reframing
3. Dry punchline or quiet realization

## Full-Page Nine-Panel Comic

Used for larger ideas with multiple beats.

Structure:

```text
[1] [2] [3]
[4] [5] [6]
[7] [8] [9]
```

Suggested rhythm:

1. Introduce technical premise
2. Human concern appears
3. First attempted solution
4. Over-explanation
5. Diagram or whiteboard escalation
6. Consequence becomes obvious
7. Quiet reflection
8. Absurd but plausible alternative
9. Understated final line
