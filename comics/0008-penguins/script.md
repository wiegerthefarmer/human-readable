# Penguins

**Format:** 3-panel strip

**Concept:** Distracting digital photos and their real-life consequences.

## Panels

**Panel 1:** Stick figure sitting at a desk, looking intently at their monitor. A picture of penguins is visible on the screen.
> "These penguins are adorable."

**Panel 2:** Stick figure now running with a briefcase in hand, hasty lines indicate rushing. Background shows a clock displaying 9:05 AM.
> "Oh no, I'm late!"

**Panel 3:** Stick figure at work, standing at a desk, with a colleague looking at them quizzically. A sticky note labeled 'Meeting - 9AM' is on their laptop.
> "Must have been the penguins."
