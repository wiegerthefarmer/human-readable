---

**Panel 4**
*Person with a lightbulb moment, open book showing icons and emoji.*
> maybe some kind of pictographic art hash to compare at-a-glance.

---

**Panel 5**
*Whiteboard diagram.*

**Pictographic Art Hash (PAH)**

`input → fn() → art hash` *(shown as emoji icons)*

- deterministic: same input → same art hash
- compact: short and scannable
- human-friendly: no reading required
- fun: ✨

---
