# FLOOMP

**Format:** 9-panel page

---

**Panel 1:** Movie theater exterior. Marquee reads "NOW PLAYING: EPIC SPACE SAGA." Character approaches, humming.

**Panel 2:** Concession stand. Character: "LARGE POPCORN, PLEASE."

**Panel 3:** Character walks into the theater, arms full of popcorn and drink. Rows of giant recliner seats visible ahead.

**Panel 4:** Character stops, stares at an enormous cushioned recliner. "UH..."

**Panel 5:** Character sits. Sound effect: "FLOOMP." They sink deep into the oversized cushions.

**Panel 6:** Character nearly swallowed by cushions, only head visible. Thought: "THIS IS..."

**Panel 7:** Character even more buried. Thought: "TOO MUCH."

**Panel 8:** Movie playing on screen — spaceship explosion in space. Character's head barely visible in the foreground, nearly submerged in cushion.

**Panel 9:** Character standing outside after the film. Thought: "IT WAS FINE, I'M NOT A HUGE FAN OF THE GIANT CUSHIONED SEATS, WHICH THEY HAD PUT ON." Poster on wall: "COMING SOON: EVEN MORE CUSHION — MAX."
