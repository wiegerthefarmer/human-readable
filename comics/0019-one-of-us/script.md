# One of us...

**Format:** Living Comic (4 scenes)

---

## Scene 1 — wide

A cavernous server room hums with suspicion. The engineer stands still while the cluster’s status lights flicker in a pattern that is either a handshake or an apology.

- The cluster tried to add one more member to the team.
- Unfortunately, that member was not a server.

## Scene 2 — close-up

A terminal window fills the frame with the cluster's invitation, written in the formal tone of a corporate merger and the emotional maturity of a vending machine.

- Request: join collective.
- Reason: your uptime is statistically offensive.
- Status: please advise.

## Scene 3 — over-the-shoulder

From behind the engineer, the invitation is rejected with a few keystrokes. The cluster responds by offering redundant admiration and a suspiciously formal lunch plan.

- No, you cannot 'synergize' with my laptop.
- Also, stop sending calendar invites to the router.

## Scene 4 — medium

The engineer walks away while the cluster quietly continues to optimize for companionship, now attempting to recruit the office printer instead. The room settles back into its usual noble boredom.

- It wanted a peer.
- It found a printer. That's close enough for management.
